# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Inventory & Payment System (v1.0.0)

## 1. Project Overview

The goal of Version 1.0.0 is to establish a foundational system for the curtain business that manages simple, unit-based stock and basic retail transactions. This version prioritizes stock accuracy and transaction logging over the complex fabrication and custom-order features detailed in the core business plan.

---

## 2. Target Audience

- **Store Owner:** To manage stock levels and view sales history.
- **Store Clerk:** To process retail transactions efficiently.

---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Simple Inventory Management

- **Unit-Based Tracking:** The system will exclusively handle items as whole units (integers). Decimal-based metered tracking is excluded from this phase.
- **Batch-Based Stock Intake:** Users must be able to add stock in "batches". Each batch must record:
  - `Quantity Received`
  - `Cost Price`
  - `Timestamp`
- **Stock History (Audit Trail):** Every movement of stock (Additions, Sales, Adjustments) must be logged in a history table to ensure accountability.

### 3.2 Automated Depletion Logic (FIFO/LIFO)

The system must manage inventory according to a global setting that determines which batch of a product is sold first:

- **FIFO (First-In, First-Out):** Sells the oldest stock first to prevent dead stock.
- **LIFO (Last-In, First-Out):** Sells the most recently received stock first.
- **Logic Execution:** When a sale occurs, the system automatically decrements the `quantity_remaining` from the eligible batch in the database.

### 3.3 Simple Payment & Sales

- **Retail Pricing:** Every product will have a single, static retail price. Wholesale tiers are excluded from v1.0.0.
- **Point of Sale (POS) Action:**
  - Select product and quantity.
  - Calculate total based on retail price.
  - Record payment method (e.g., Cash or Digital).
  - Generate a unique Transaction ID.

---

## 4. Technical Requirements & Data Schema

### 4.1 Database Design (Conceptual)

- **Products Table:** `id`, `name`, `sku`, `retail_price`.
- **Stock_Batches Table:** `id`, `product_id`, `quantity_remaining`, `cost_price`, `created_at`.
- **Transactions Table:** `id`, `total_amount`, `payment_method`, `created_at`.
- **Stock_Ledger Table:** `id`, `batch_id`, `transaction_id`, `change_amount` (positive for intake, negative for sales).

### 4.2 System Settings

- **Depletion Toggle:** A global configuration setting to switch between FIFO and LIFO logic.

---

## 5. User Workflow

1.  **Inventory Setup:** Owner adds 20 units of "Curtain Rod A" (Batch 001).
2.  **Inventory Update:** Next week, Owner adds 10 more units (Batch 002).
3.  **Sales Transaction:** Clerk sells 5 units.
4.  **Backend Calculation:**
    - If **FIFO**: System removes 5 units from Batch 001.
    - If **LIFO**: System removes 5 units from Batch 002.
5.  **Payment Completion:** System records the revenue and updates the Stock History.

---

## 6. Out of Scope (Future Versions)

- **Meters/Decimals:** No support for fabric-by-the-meter or fractional cuts.
- **Bill of Materials (BOM):** No custom-product fabrication logic.
- **Wholesale Pricing:** No multi-tier pricing for different customer types.
- **Warehouse Assignments:** Single-location stock management only.
